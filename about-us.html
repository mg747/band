Early Life and Career Beginnings:

Already distinctive powers on their own, the combination of Natty James (tango) and Kama Don (kash) formed an Irish Alternative Hip-Hop group later to be known as Def Bond in Dublin 2008. Tango a rapper and producer and Kash a vocalist were childhood friends raised in Malahide, suburban Dublin. They started skateboarding and participating in street battle rap at a very young age. Shortly after the duo had developed their skills and well known nationally, they were invited to rap live on record producer and music executive DJ Bling radio show. DJ Bling signed them to his Mega Jam Record Label. In 2009 they relocated to California to record their debut album. In 2014, Tango mentioned in an interview with Complix Magazine that the first two years of their career was the toughest, because fame had lead them to fast life in Los Angeles, that almost affected our creative process of making quality music.

MUSIC

Def Bond released their first full-lenght album "FADED" in April 2010. It included colaboration with artists like Big Louch, Glazer Boiz and Chanel Seven. It debuted at No. 3 at the Billboard Hot 100, and sold over 360,000 copies in its first week. The album went platinum in a matter of weeks, and recieved a Fanny nomination, and also spawned hits such as "Daytona," "Levitate," and "Break Free." In 2011, Def Bond followed up the success of their first album with WILD WEST, which was massively successful, debuting at No. 1 on the Billboard charts, selling 410,000 in its first week, and has gone double platinum since its release. The Album also won them their first Fanny Award for rap album of the year. Three years elapsed between the release of WILD WEST and their third studio album ANTHEM but they continued to collaborate with other artists and formed their own record label, TECK Entertainment, which is currently distributed by Global Grind Records. When Anthem was released in september 2014, It went No. 1 on the Billboard charts making it their second No. 1 album, and sold 470,000 copies in its first week. The album has gone platinum ever since. Their new album Immortal was released in march 14 2021.

PHILANTROPHY

Def Bond is active in giving back, most notably through their philantropic work through the Gogetter Foundation. The foundation, founded in 2016, is dedicated to helping individuals facing social-economical hardship further their education, and encouraging them to tap into their potential.
